2018/2019

Water robot researcher

  • December
  • /
  • March

I presented for a multicultural exchage the prototype of a drone powered by arduino able to recognize water point on the upper layer of sandy areas like desert. The drone requires two Arduinos, the first to control movement and the second to process and store the data collected by the connected sensors. the mechanics are very complicated to explain, the robot never worked on a practical level but I will probably try to develop the idea in the future.
